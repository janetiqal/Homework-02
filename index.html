About Me

Hi, my name is Janet Iqal. I graduated from Loyola University Chicago with a bachelors degree in Biology...so how did I end up here? I minored in Bio-Statistics my last year in school and we dabbled in programs like R and used coding to model data from large data sets. That exposure made me put my plans on hold for pharmacy school.I went into EMS to figure out if I should continue with a career in medicine. Unfortunately for the field of medicine, I decided to take my talents to become a full stack web developer. The seed was planted years ago and I am grateful because here I am--building my portfolio page, showing you all the fun things I have learned to do in such a short time.
